Ultrasound of wrist and hand masses.

Ultrasound is a useful tool to investigate soft tissue masses in the wrist and
hand. In most situations ultrasound helps distinguish between a cyst and a tissue
mass. This article provides a simple clinical approach to the use of ultrasound
imaging for the diagnosis and preoperative assessment of wrist and hand masses.
